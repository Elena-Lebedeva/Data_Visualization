# Data Visualization & Social Impact Portfolio

This repository contains documentation and direct access to high-leverage Business Intelligence projects developed for global non-profit organizations. These dashboards translate complex operational and sociological datasets into actionable insights to drive policy change, resource allocation, and climate resilience.

## 🏆 Featured Showcases

### 1. Bhumi: Empowering Change Through Volunteering
**Stakeholder:** Bhumi (Top Indian NGO focused on education and community welfare)
**Recognition:** *Selected by Bhumi for the "Data Change Makers" Showcase.*
* **Objective:** Visualizing financial transparency and volunteer impact to drive community welfare.
* **Link:** [View Interactive Dashboard](https://datastudio.google.com/s/tE6szt536To)

### 2. Advancing Torture Prevention (1984–2024)
**Stakeholder:** Association for the Prevention of Torture (APT)
